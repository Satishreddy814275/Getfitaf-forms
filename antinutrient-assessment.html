Anti-Nutrient Load Assessment

About This Assessment

Anti-nutrients are factors in your daily environment - not just your food - that interfere with your body's ability to absorb and use essential nutrients. This includes pollution, medication, stress, alcohol, and food quality. Answer honestly to get an accurate picture of your toxic load and a personalised action plan.

How to score: Answer Yes or No to each question. Each "Yes" = 1 point. The ideal score is 0. A score of 5 or more indicates a significant anti-nutrient load that is likely impacting your health and results.

The 11 Questions

Select Yes or No for each. Take your time - honesty gives you better results.

  • 1 Do you regularly drink tap water?
  • 2 Is more than half of the food you eat non-organic?
  • 3 Do you spend an hour or more per day in heavy traffic?
  • 4 Do you live or work in a city or area with significant air pollution?
  • 5 Do you smoke, or are you regularly around people who smoke?
  • 6 Do you often eat fried food (deep fried, pan fried, or sautéed in a lot of oil)?
  • 7 Do you eat processed or packaged food every day?
  • 8 Do you take more than 20 painkillers (aspirin, paracetamol, or ibuprofen) in a year?
  • 9 Do you take one or more courses of antibiotics per year?
  • 10 Is most of the food or drink you consume in contact with soft plastic or regular cling film?
  • 11 Do you have an alcoholic drink on most days?

📋 Your Personalised Action Plan
💊 Core Supplements - Recommended for Everyone

Anti-nutrients don't just block absorption of vitamins and minerals from your food - they actively deplete what your body already has. These three supplements form the foundation for counteracting that load, regardless of your score.

  • 🌿 High-quality Multivitamin + Mineral - Take daily with food. Choose a comprehensive formula that covers B vitamins, zinc, magnesium, selenium, and iron. This covers the nutritional gaps created by anti-nutrients in your environment and diet.
  • 🍊 Vitamin C - 1,000mg daily - One of the most powerful natural detoxifiers. Vitamin C neutralises over 50 harmful environmental substances (including exhaust fumes, heavy metals, and cigarette toxins), supports immune function, and is rapidly depleted by any form of stress - physical, chemical, or emotional.
  • 🐟 Omega 3 & 6 Fatty Acids - daily - Essential fats your body cannot produce on its own. They maintain healthy cell membranes (your first line of defence against toxins entering cells), support brain function, reduce inflammation, and help your body absorb fat-soluble vitamins.
💡 Important Things to Know
You can be overfed but still malnourished. If your anti-nutrient load is high, your body may be unable to absorb the nutrients from the food you eat - even if your diet looks healthy. This is why addressing your toxic load is just as important as improving your diet.
Anti-nutrients don't only come from food. Tap water, pollution, medication, alcohol, smoking, plastic packaging, and chronic stress all act as anti-nutrients by depleting or blocking vitamins and minerals. Cleaning up your diet alone may not be enough.
Standard supplement doses may not be enough for you. The recommended daily allowances (RDAs) listed on supplement labels assume a baseline healthy environment. If your anti-nutrient load is elevated, your actual need for key nutrients - especially Vitamin C - can be significantly higher than the standard RDA.
Small changes compound quickly. You don't need to fix everything at once. Addressing just 2-3 of your highest-impact Yes answers can meaningfully reduce your toxic load within weeks. Your coach will help you prioritise.
